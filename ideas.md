## 🛠️ Upcoming Features & Feedback (In Progress)

### 🕹️ Gameplay & Mechanics
- **Manager Mode**: No explanation needed...
- **Avatar Creator**: Rudimentary system to customise your player/manager appearance.
- **Rivalry System**: Unique pre-match events generated specifically for "Derby" days or high-stakes matches.
- **Youth Academy Connection**: Mentored players can now graduate to the first team, granting the player a **Loyalty Bonus**.

### 📈 Progression & Economy
- **More Investment Options**: Expanded ways to spend and grow your career earnings.
- **Contract Release Clauses**: Set your own price. Offers are automatically triggered when your OVR meets the threshold.

### 🏥 Realism & Systems
- **Weather System**: Dynamic pitch conditions.
    - *Frozen Pitch*: Lower scoring games.
    - *Wet Pitch*: Increased error rate and unpredictable ball physics.
- **Physio Progress Bar**: Detailed ACL rehab tracking. Includes month-by-month recovery and mini-decisions that impact recovery speed.
